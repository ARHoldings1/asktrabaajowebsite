Our Journey: From Humble Beginnings to Global Leadership

Founded in 2016, Trabaajo has grown from a Mumbai startup to a global HR tech powerhouse.

2017

We expanded to Singapore and won the prestigious "HR Technology of the Year" award at the World HR Congress.

2018

Trabaajo made its mark on the global stage as finalists at both Startup Istanbul and Startup Turkey.

2019

We were a proud participant at Web Summit, Louisiana, where we established our first Centres of Excellence in four U.S. universities and one in Singapore.

2023

A landmark year! Trabaajo Inc. became a proud part of AR Holdings Inc. , a global business conglomerate known for its disruptive approach across industries.

2024

Trabaajo launched its own blockchain, setting up Centres of Excellence in Thailand and expanding operations to Vietnam, Malaysia, the Philippines, and New Zealand. This was also the year we unveiled the world's first complete HR tech ecosystem powered by a proprietary AI engine, voice assistant, and blockchain technology.

Today, Trabaajo spans across the globe, with operations in India, Singapore, the USA, Thailand, Australia, Japan, Amsterdam, Dubai, Vietnam, Malaysia, the Philippines, and New Zealand. From humble beginnings to a global presence, we remain committed to redefining HR technology with innovation and purpose.

Meet Our Founder: Arun Kumar

Visionary Leader, Innovator, and Architect of Tomorrow's Businesses

"Entrepreneurship is not just about taking risks; it's about taking responsibility. The world doesn't wait for you to succeed—you have to earn your place with bold ideas and relentless and shameless execution." – Arun Kumar

Arun Kumar, the founder of Trabaajo Inc. and AR Holdings Inc., is a trailblazer in the world of entrepreneurship and innovation. From his early days in Mumbai to leading globally recognized companies, Arun has always been driven by a singular vision—to create businesses that disrupt industries and add value to people's lives.

Arun's journey began in 2016 when he founded Trabaajo Inc., a groundbreaking HR tech startup in Mumbai, India. With a clear mission to redefine how organizations manage human resources, he built Trabaajo as a platform that empowers all stakeholders in the HR ecosystem—from recruiters to job seekers.

Under his leadership, Trabaajo grew rapidly, expanding to Singapore in 2017 and subsequently entering major global markets like the USA, Thailand, Australia, Japan, Amsterdam, Dubai, and more. Arun's forward-thinking vision helped Trabaajo become the world's first HR tech ecosystem powered by AI, a voice assistant, and blockchain technology, setting a new standard for data security, process automation, and user engagement in HR.

In 2023, Arun Kumar led Trabaajo's strategic integration into AR Holdings Inc., a diverse conglomerate disrupting industries such as blockchain, logistics, hospitality, FMCG, and construction. As the founder of AR Holdings, Arun has created a dynamic group of companies unified by their commitment to innovation, sustainability, and excellence.

Beyond his role as a business leader, Arun Kumar is an influential voice in the world of entrepreneurship. He is a sought-after guest speaker at global conferences and renowned business schools, where he shares his expertise on topics such as strategic thinking, scaling businesses through innovation, and entrepreneurial resilience.
